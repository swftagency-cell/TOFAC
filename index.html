Do You Want To Give Your Life To Christ?

Please Follow The Steps Below:

Acknowledge all your sins — Acts 2:36–38

Recognize the need for salvation and turn your heart to God with sincerity.

Confess those sins — Galatians 5:19–21

Openly confess and forsake sinful works, choosing the path of righteousness.

Ask for forgiveness of sin — 1 John 1:9

God is faithful and just to forgive and cleanse from all unrighteousness.

Repent of those sins — Acts 3:19

Turn away from sin and be renewed, that times of refreshing may come.

Forsake old ways and sinful habits — Luke 14:33

Embrace a new life in Christ, surrendering fully and walking in obedience.

Join a Bible-believing church — Hebrews 10:25

Fellowship strengthens faith. Connect with a local church to grow together.
