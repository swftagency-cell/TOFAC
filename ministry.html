Our Story

Founded with a vision to welcome all, Truth of Faith Church (TOFAC) has grown into a vibrant community committed to faith, service, and compassion. Our journey has been marked by the steady growth of ministries, outreach, and a passion for God’s word.

Our Mission

We strive to be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community and beyond—creating a place where people of all backgrounds can encounter God, grow in their faith, and discover purpose.
